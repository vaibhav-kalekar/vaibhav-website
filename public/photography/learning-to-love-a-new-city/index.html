Learning to Love a new City

The beautiful city of Sydney and the ways it is making me fall in love with it
November 17, 2025 · 1 min · 96 words · Me | Suggest Changes

After moving from one beloved city I called home to a completely new one that became a second home, and then leaving that to move and settle into an entirely unknown city can take its toll. I am fortunate enough to call some of the most beautiful cities in the world as my home - Mumbai to Chicago to Munich and now Sydney.

I am learning the ways of Sydney and Australia, yet it has been such a smooth transition to be swayed by the charm of this beautiful city.
